Precautions for Different Animals

Dogs
Rabies, Distemper, Parvovirus, Adenovirus, Parainfluenza, Bordetella, Lyme disease.
Annual vet visits for health screening and parasite control.
Balanced diet and regular exercise to maintain a healthy weight.
Cats
Rabies, Feline Viral Rhinotracheitis, Calicivirus, Panleukopenia, Feline Leukemia Virus.
Annual vet visits for health screening and parasite control.
Balanced diet and regular exercise to prevent obesity.
Chickens
Marekâ€™s disease, Newcastle disease, Infectious Bronchitis, Fowl Pox.
Clean and dry living environment to prevent diseases.
Balanced diet with necessary vitamins and minerals.
Monkeys
Measles, Rabies, Tetanus, Hepatitis A and B.
Regular vet visits for health monitoring and parasite control.
Provide mental and physical stimulation to prevent boredom and stress.
Turtles
Clean and temperature-controlled aquatic environment.
Regular cleaning of habitat to prevent shell and skin infections.
Balanced diet with necessary calcium and vitamins.
Birds
Avian Influenza, Newcastle disease (depending on species).
Clean and spacious cage with proper ventilation.
Balanced diet with necessary vitamins and minerals.
Sheep
Clostridial diseases, Footrot, Rabies (if necessary).
Rotational grazing to prevent overgrazing and parasite build-up.
Regular shearing to maintain hygiene and prevent heat stress.
Goats
Clostridial diseases, Rabies (if necessary), Pneumonia.
Rotational grazing to maintain pasture health and reduce parasites.
Regular hoof trimming to prevent overgrowth and lameness.
Ducks
Duck Viral Enteritis, Avian Influenza (if necessary).
Clean and dry living environment with access to water for swimming.
Balanced diet with necessary vitamins and minerals.
